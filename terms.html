This Actual Budget installation is a private, non-commercial personal-finance application intended exclusively for use by its operator. No financial or software service is offered to third parties.

Bank connections

The operator may authorise Enable Banking and participating financial institutions to provide account and transaction information to this application. Only accounts personally authorised by the operator may be connected.

Availability and accuracy

The application is provided for personal use without any guarantee of uninterrupted availability, completeness or accuracy. Imported information should be checked against records supplied directly by the relevant financial institution.

Withdrawal of access

Banking access may be withdrawn through the relevant financial institution or Enable Banking. Associated information may also be deleted from Actual Budget.
